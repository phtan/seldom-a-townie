Smaller book-stores that I have been to in Singapore

21 Oct 2018

By ‘smaller bookstores’ I mean bookstores that, in a sense, aren’t like Kinokuniya, Times, Popular and so on.

I list the bookstores with their brick-and-mortar addresses as well as their web-addresses.

  1. ‘The Moon’. 37 Mosque Street. https://www.facebook.com/themoonsg
  2. ‘BooksActually’. 9 Yong Siak Street, Singapore 168645. https://www.booksactuallyshop.com/pages/about
  3. ‘City Book Room’. 420 North Bridge Road, #03-10, North Bridge Centre, Singapore 188727. https://www.facebook.com/citybookroomsingapore/

Not forgetting a travelling bookstore owned by Anthony:

  • ‘Booktique’. https://www.facebook.com/WriterBooktique/
