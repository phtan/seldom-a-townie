Places where I have used my laptop

08 Oct 2018

  2. ‘Enchanted’. URL: https://www.facebook.com/EnchantedCafeSg/
  3. ‘Old Hen’. URL: https://www.facebook.com/oldhencoffee/
  4. ‘Coffeesmith’. https://www.facebook.com/CoffeesmithSG/
