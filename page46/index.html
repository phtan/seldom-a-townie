Places where I have used my laptop

08 Oct 2018

I have had the luxury and privilege of using my laptop at the following places, where beverages are sold

(I am currently residing in Singapore - which is in South-east Asia, if you are not familiar with her.)

  1. ‘Brunches’. URL: https://www.facebook.com/BrunchesCafe/
  2. ‘Enchanted’. Have charged my laptop at the power-sockets there. URL: https://www.facebook.com/EnchantedCafeSg/
  3. ‘Old Hen’. Have charged my laptop there. URL: https://www.facebook.com/oldhencoffee/
  4. ‘Coffeesmith’. https://www.facebook.com/CoffeesmithSG/
  5. ‘Kopi & Tarts’, in the shopping mall City Square. Have charged my laptop there. https://kopiandtarts.com.sg/
  6. ‘Real Food’, in the shopping mall Orchard Central. https://www.realfoodgrocer.com/

I had the inspiration to post about this after visiting Tania Rascia’s record of cafes that she has visited: https://taniarascia.github.io/coffee/

And of course I can only have such inspiration if my god gives it to me. I thank Him for that.

An update on twenty-first of October, 2018 A.D.

  2. ‘Symmetry’. http://www.symmetry.com.sg/
