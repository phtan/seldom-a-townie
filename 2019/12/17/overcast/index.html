Recipe for a (Milo-based) cocktail

I got this recipe from a bartender at Bar Stories , located on the island of Singapore.

It is based on Milo, a non-alcoholic beverage which is kinda based on malt. (I am aware, from anecdotal evidence, that Milo is popular, if not well-known, in at least the South-east Asian countries of Malaysia and Singapore).
